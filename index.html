About me

I am currently working with the Department of Computer Science, University of Exeter, UK. Before joining the current group, I worked and studied at the University of Otago, New Zealand, and Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (BUAA), Beijing, China.

My background is complex since my Bachelor and Master degrees were granted in Mechanical Design, Manufacture and Its Automation, and Control Science and Engineering in Software Reliability, respectively. I spent a lot of effort and insistence to explore new things when I started a new research direction. However, the multidisciplinary background inspires me to think different in my research work.
