About me

I am currently working with the Department of Computer Science, University of Exeter, UK. Before joining the current group, I worked and studied at the University of Otago, New Zealand, and Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Beijing, China.

My Master and Bachelor degrees are granted in Control Science and Engineering (Software Reliability and Safety in Aircraft Control Systems) and Mechanical Design, Manufacture and Its Automation, respectively.
