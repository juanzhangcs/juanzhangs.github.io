About me

I received the B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in computer engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST), and the University of Tehran, Iran, in 2005 and 2008, respectively. In April 2015, I received the PhD degree in Telecommunications under the supervision of Prof. Mikael Johansson and Prof. Carlo Fischione at KTH.

Research

My research interests include

  • Convex Optimisation

  • Distributed Algorithms

  • Real-time Sensor Networking

  • Wireless Networks

  • Machine Learning
